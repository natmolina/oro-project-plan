What we're building

A simple, professional 4-page business website for Oro Woodshop, along with a light rebrand to support the latest evolution of Oro. No more Home Depot orange! Less "construction-core" and camo, more refined craft and sustainable woodwork. The site will be easy to use and purposeful: People can browse your portfolio, get inspired for their own projects, learn about you, and reach out with inquiries. And while there's no e-commerce plan included this time, the website will set the foundatin for an online store down the road.

Site Outline
Home
  • Short intro + big hero photo at the top, cycling through a few of your best shots
  • Portfolio preview grid
  • Testimonials in a little carousel
  • Persistent elements include navigation, and Instagram/link to contact form on the footer of every page
Work
  • Full project grid: all projects
  • 2–3 featured projects with process photos and a bit of the story behind them
  • Rest are photos + short caption
About
  • Your name + professional bio
  • Photo or illustration, I'll mock something up
  • Sustainability note + mission/values
Contact
  • Form: collects person's name, email, optional phone, message
  • Copy that makes clear you take commissions
  • Instagram link
